Tree like data storing

This is mainly a client side site where you can store, view, manage data in proper tree like format. Think it like, you want to track data about your family and relatives(i.e, your grand father's family, their brothers or sisters, their sons and so on).

In this example , your grand father's father will be main parent. Then, his sons/daughters will be parent for their descandants. It can go upto as high as you want. So, this tree will make you be clear and make you remember whose children belongs to which parent and so on.

Now, you have understood the concept, lets talk about facilities and how to use it. There is black box at the top of the page, it represents one element in a tree. If you click, there you will see:

1. :- To add a child

2. :- To view the content of the box

3. :- Expand the box's children if present

4. :- Delete the box

*Note:- There is button if you click inside button. There is button to hide the expanded children of box(It will be shown only if the box is expanded already).

The link between box is called branch and box at other end below is its direct child and so on below will be its grand child. you can create a complete tree by adding boxes and linking between them. boxes with branches between has direct link .

At the top right corner there are two buttons, one button and just by the side, there is like icon, we call it notes.

button will show you three main functional buttons and a help button at the buttom of the three buttons.

1. :- To upload the created tree in cloud(server).

2. :- To clear the workspace(mistaken tree structure).

3. :- To clone trees with provided key(while saving you will get or in your notes inside notification tab)

Note has two tab,

1. Action:- Your request status

2. Notification:- Your tree data saved in cloud

At the bottom right corner, you can see three butttons(Zoom feature).

1. :- To zoomin

2. :- To reset zoom

3. :- To zoom out.
